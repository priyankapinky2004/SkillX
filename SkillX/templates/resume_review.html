Resume Writing Tips

Content Tips
  • Use action verbs to start bullet points
  • Include quantifiable achievements
  • Customize for each job application
  • Focus on relevant experience and skills
  • Keep it concise and impactful
Format Tips
  • Use a clean, professional template
  • Ensure consistent formatting throughout
  • Limit to 1-2 pages maximum
  • Use a readable font (Arial, Calibri, etc.)
  • Save and send as a PDF to preserve formatting
ATS Optimization Tips
  • Include keywords from the job description
  • Use standard section headings
  • Avoid tables, headers/footers, or text boxes
  • Don't put important information in images
  • Use a simple, straightforward layout
