Interview Tips

Research common interview questions for your target role and practice your responses. Focus on clear, concise answers with concrete examples from your experience.

For behavioral questions, structure your answers using the STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, and Result. This helps you provide complete, focused responses.

Understand the company's mission, values, products, and culture. This demonstrates your interest and helps you align your answers with their objectives.

Prepare 3-5 insightful questions to ask the interviewer about the role, team, or company. This shows your engagement and helps you evaluate if the position is right for you.

What is the difference between let, const, and var in JavaScript?

Sample Answer

Let and const are block-scoped, while var is function-scoped. Const cannot be reassigned once declared, whereas let and var can be. Var is hoisted to the top of its scope with an initial value of undefined, but let and const are hoisted without initialization, resulting in a "temporal dead zone". Use const by default, let when you need to reassign, and avoid var in modern code.
